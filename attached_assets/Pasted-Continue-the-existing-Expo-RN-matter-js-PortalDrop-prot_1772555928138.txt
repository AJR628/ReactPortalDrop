NEW GAMEPLAY RULE (authoritative)
- While the puck is running, EVERY TAP alternates which portal is moved:
  Tap #1 moves Entry, Tap #2 moves Exit, Tap #3 moves Entry, etc.
- After a teleport occurs, the FIRST tap should move the portal the puck JUST CAME OUT OF.
  In current code, you set entryPortal = previous exitPortal on teleport, so:
  After teleport: set nextPortalToMove = 'entry' (so first tap moves the just-exited portal).
- Both portals remain visible and active at all times (as currently rendered).
